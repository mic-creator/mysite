Fuel Types

Premium Motor Spirit (PMS)

Premium Motor Spirit, commomly known as gasoline or petro, ia a fuel used in spark ignition enegines. It is suitable for most standard automobalies.

  • High energy content
  • Optimal engine performance

Automotive Gas Oil

Automotive Gas Oil, also known as diesel fuel, is primarily used in compression ignition engines. It is suitable for trucks buses and other diesel-powered vechiles.

  • Higher energy density
  • Efficient and economical fuel consumption
  • Appropriate for heavy-duty applications
